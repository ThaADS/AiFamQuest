### Brand Personality

**Visual Elements**:
- Rounded corners (friendly, approachable)
- Soft shadows (depth without harshness)
- Bright colors (energetic, positive)
- Ample whitespace (calm, organized)
- Playful animations (fun, engaging)

**Avoided**:
- Sharp edges (too corporate)
- Harsh shadows (too serious)
- Muted colors (too boring)
- Cramped layouts (too overwhelming)
- No animations (too static)
