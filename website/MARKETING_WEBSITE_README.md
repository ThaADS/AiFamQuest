## ğŸ­ Tone & Voice

### Do's âœ…
- **Relatable**: Use real mom struggles ("Voor de 3e keer vragen...")
- **Humor**: Light, self-aware ("spoiler: het is niet gedaan")
- **Empathy**: Acknowledge the mental load
- **Confident**: Solution-focused, not preachy
- **Casual**: Friendly, like a smart friend

### Don'ts âŒ
- **No marketing jargon**: "synergize", "revolutionary", "game-changer"
- **No guilt-tripping**: Don't blame moms for struggles
- **No over-promising**: Be honest about what AI can/can't do
- **No condescension**: Respect the hard work moms do

### Example Copy

**Good**: "Eindelijk ruzie-vrij de tafel afruimen! Mijn kids vechten nu om wie de vaatwasser mag uitruimen."

**Bad**: "Revolutionize your family dynamics with our cutting-edge gamification platform that synergizes AI-powered task distribution."
